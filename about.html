        Hi, I'm David Grimshaw. I'm a web developer with an education in graphic design and illustration. I've had a long-time interest in electronics and computer hardware, and have now combined those interests and that experience with coding. I believe in logical, functional design that aims for a straightforward and easy user experience. I am constantly building experience with new frameworks, so I can find what will work best with various situations through practical experience.
        Outside of coding and electronics interests, I work as an illustrator and have had illustrations published by various outlets such as Road & Track, Popular Mechanics, and Jalopnik. I specialize in automotive illustration and cartooning. All images on this site were created by myself.
